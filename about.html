Hey there, I’m

SOUMYARANJAN BEHERA

I’m a passionate UI/UX designer based in Bhubaneswar, India. I love creating beautiful and interactive designs that captures the eyes of users. I’m currently completing my 2nd year in CSE at Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology.

With a knack for creating intuitive & delightful user experiences. I blend creativity with technical skills to craft interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional.

I believe that great design is a balance of form and function. It's not just about making things look good but ensuring they work seamlessly for the user. My design process is deeply user-centric, involving extensive research, wireframing, prototyping, and user testing. I strive to understand the user's needs, pain points, and behaviors to create solutions that truly resonate with them.
