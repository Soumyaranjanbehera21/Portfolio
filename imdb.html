Brief

IMDb (Internet Movie Database) is an online database of information related to films, television series, podcasts, home videos, video games, and streaming content online – including cast, production crew and personal biographies, plot summaries, trivia, ratings, and fan and critical reviews.

Problem

Overcrowded Navigation: The navbar contains an excessive number of links, making it difficult for users to quickly find the information they need.

Lack of Visual Hierarchy: The current design fails to effectively guide users through the content, leading to a confusing and overwhelming user experience.

Information Overload: Pages are densely packed with information, often presenting more data than necessary, which can overwhelm and disorient users.

Outdated Design: The overall aesthetic of the website feels dated, potentially failing to resonate with younger audiences or those accustomed to more modern web designs.

Poor Content Organization: The lack of clear content structure makes it challenging for users to efficiently navigate and find desired information.

Goal

Modernize the visual design of IMDb
Improve overall user experience and navigation
Streamline content presentation
